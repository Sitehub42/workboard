About Work Board

Our mission: To bridge the gap between skilled job seekers and deserving employers across South Africa, fostering growth and success for all.

Work Board specializes in Human Resources, leveraging our deep understanding of the job market and cutting-edge candidate screening processes. We pride ourselves on creating a seamless experience for both parties, ensuring the right talent finds the right role.

We are dedicated to client success, employing agile methodologies to adapt to the evolving demands of the job market and continuously refine our platform. Our focus is on efficiency, reliability, and ultimately, making meaningful connections that drive careers and business growth.

Our Services

Work Board offers a comprehensive suite of HR and platform services designed to streamline the recruitment process for both job seekers and employers.

Candidate Screening

Thoroughly vetting candidates to ensure they meet employer requirements and possess the necessary skills and experience.

Job Seeker Database Platform

A robust platform where job seekers register, answer screening questions, and get added to the Work Board database.

Employer Candidate Search/Request

Employers can efficiently search our database for suitable candidates or request pre-screened individuals tailored to their needs.

WhatsApp for Communication

Utilizing WhatsApp for seamless interviews, updates, and follow-up questions, ensuring quick and direct communication.
